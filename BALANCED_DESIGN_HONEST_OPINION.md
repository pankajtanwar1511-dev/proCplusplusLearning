## Version 1: Flashy (Over-designed)

### What It Looked Like:
```

║ 🌊🌊🌊 Code Examples 🌊🌊🌊                    ║ ← Teal banner
╚═══════════════════════════════════════════════╝
```

### Honest Assessment:

**Pros:**
- ✅ Shows effort and craftsmanship
- ✅ Looks impressive in screenshots
- ✅ Feels "premium"

**Cons:**
- ❌ **Too much** - like wearing all your jewelry at once
- ❌ **Exhausting** - tiring after 10 minutes
- ❌ **Wasteful** - duplicate title wastes space
- ❌ **Trying too hard** - screams "LOOK HOW DESIGNED I AM!"
- ❌ **Designer ego** - showing off skills instead of serving user

**My Honest Rating: 6/10**
- Impressive but impractical
- Good for portfolio, bad for learning

## The Key Differences

### Balanced Design Philosophy:

**ONE accent color** (teal) used in TWO places:
1. Small number badge (8x8 pixels)
2. Thin vertical line (1px x 20px)

Total teal pixels: ~100 pixels
**Just enough to add personality, not enough to overwhelm**

### Strategic Accent Placement:
```
[1]  ← Accent #1: Shows progression
 │
 │
 ↓
│ How It Works  ← Accent #2: Highlights explanation
```

Both accents **serve a purpose**:
- Number badge: Navigation aid
- Vertical line: Content structure

Not decoration for decoration's sake!
